Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bites began as a small bistro with a big dream - to redefine casual dining. Our chef-owner brings 20 years of experience from Michelin-starred kitchens around the world.

We believe in sustainable sourcing, supporting local farmers, and creating dishes that tell a story. Every plate is a celebration of flavors, textures, and culinary innovation.
